The City of No Love

Chapter 2 — Blood Oaths

By sunrise the streets already belonged to someone.

Marcus knew every corner had eyes watching him. Every move could start a war.

The organizations had one rule—betrayal was paid for in blood.

A black SUV rolled beside him. The window lowered. One sentence changed everything.

"The King wants to see you."

Marcus didn't answer. He simply opened the door and climbed inside.
