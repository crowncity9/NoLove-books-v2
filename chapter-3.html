The City of No Love

Chapter 3 — The Meeting

The SUV stopped outside an abandoned warehouse overlooking the river.

Marcus stepped out without speaking. Armed guards watched every entrance.

Inside, a single man sat at the end of a long table.

People called him The King. No one knew his real name.

"The city is changing," the King said quietly. "And I need someone willing to get their hands dirty."

Marcus stared back without fear. "What do you want me to do?"

The King smiled. "Start a war."
