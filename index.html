The Story

Every promise becomes a debt.

Malik Carter only wanted to protect his family. Then a dying man placed an encrypted drive in his hand and told him the truth about the uncle everyone said had run away. Before sunrise, the city’s four most powerful leaders are watching him—and someone is rewriting his life one record at a time.
